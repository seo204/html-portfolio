Best Movies Ever

Interstellar

This is a great movie because one of its producers is actually an atom physicist and many phenomenons that are depicted in the movie are actually very close to reality.

The Matrix

It is an elaboration of many philosophical approaches to human existence within a fantastic digital world.

Bruce Almighty

It is an extremely funny movie with the great acting skills of Jim Carrey. It is about a journalist who blames God for all his failures, and God gives him all his powers so that he could try doing better than Him.
